### Design System
- **Font**: Playfair Display (headlines), Inter (body)
- **Colors**: #c97d7d (dusty rose), #faf5f0 (cream), #4a3030 (dark text), #8c6b6b (secondary)
- **Style**: Magazine editorial — think Cup of Jo, Domino magazine
- **Device frame**: Optional. If used, iPhone 16 Pro Max in cream/starlight
- **Text overlays**: Must be localized per locale (separate screenshot set)
