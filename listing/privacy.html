Privacy Policy — Endopath

Last updated: 16 May 2026

1. Who we are

Endopath is built by App Factory Studio. Reach us at hello@appfactory.studio for any privacy question, or legal@appfactory.studio for formal data requests.

2. What this app collects

Nothing leaves your device. All your data — symptoms, flares, body-map entries, medications, cycle records, notes — is stored locally on your device. We do not run a server that holds your data. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal information.

We do not require an account. We do not ask for your name, email, date of birth, or any other identifying information to use the app.

3. What we don't do

  • We do not sell your data to third parties.
  • We do not run third-party advertising SDKs.
  • We do not share your data with data brokers, employers, insurers, or anyone else.
  • We do not use analytics that personally identify you.
  • We do not profile you based on your symptoms or cycle.

4. Third-party services

If you purchase Endopath Pro, the transaction is handled by Apple App Store or Google Play. Those platforms have their own privacy policies. We never see your payment details.

5. Health data

Endopath helps you track health information including pelvic pain, fatigue, mood, flow, and medication. We treat this as the most sensitive category of personal data.

  • Health data stays on your device. We have no way to read it.
  • We do not share health data with any third party.
  • We do not use health data for advertising, profiling, or research.
  • You can delete all data at any time from the Settings screen, or by uninstalling the app.
  • If you export a PDF summary for a clinician, the file lives on your device and goes only where you choose to send it.

Endopath is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified clinician for medical decisions.

6. Your rights

You can access, correct, delete, or export your data at any time from the app's Settings screen. To make a formal request, email legal@appfactory.studio.

7. Children

Endopath is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Security

Because your data never leaves your device, the main protection is your device itself — keep it locked with a passcode or biometric. We do not transmit your data, so there is no server-side breach risk. If you choose to export a PDF or share a file, that file becomes subject to wherever you send it.

9. Changes to this policy

If we materially change this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date and notify you in-app where reasonable. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance.
